Saebyul Park

K-pop Singer-Songwriter and AI Researcher

Biography | Publications | YouTube Channel | Wikipedia

Biography

  • Profession: Singer-songwriter, AI researcher, educator
  • Education: Bachelor in Psychology from Yonsei University; Master and PhD from KAIST Graduate School of Culture Technology
  • Current Affiliation: Antenna Music
  • Music Career:
    • EP "DIARY" (2008), albums "새벽별" (2010), "하이힐" (2012), "Ballades Op.3" (2019)
    • Singles "노래할게요" (2013), "SKY HIGH" (2015)
    • New EP "Everblooming" (2023)
    • Composed hit songs for Jeong Seung-hwan and Lyn
  • Media Roles:
    • Hosted "난장" (Gwangju MBC, 2009-2010)
    • DJ on EBS "아름다운 동요세상" (2011-2012)
    • Music teacher on EBS's "딩동댕 유치원" (2020-2021)
  • Academia:
    • Former adjunct professor at Baekseok and Hongik University
    • Adjunct professor at Yonsei University's Underwood International College
